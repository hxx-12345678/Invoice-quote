# Privacy Policy for Quotiq

**Last Updated: April 26, 2026**

At Quotiq, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Enterprise CPQ & Invoicing Platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, phone number.
- **Business Profile**: Legal name, tax IDs (GSTIN/VAT), business address, bank details.
- **Customer Data**: Names, emails, and addresses of your customers.

### 1.2 Usage Data
- IP addresses, browser type, device information, and activity logs.

## 2. Lawful Basis for Processing (GDPR)
We process your data under the following legal bases:
- **Contractual Necessity**: To provide the invoicing and CPQ services you signed up for.
- **Legitimate Interests**: To improve our platform and ensure security.
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with tax and financial reporting laws.

## 3. Data Subject Rights
Under GDPR, you have the following rights:
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data.
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
- **Right to Data Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Right to Object**: Object to certain types of processing.

You can exercise these rights directly through your Dashboard or by contacting privacy@quotiq.com.

## 4. Data Security
We implement enterprise-grade security measures, including:
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest.
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit.
- Strict SOC 2 compliant audit logging.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your data as long as your account is active or as required by law (e.g., tax records for 7 years).

## 6. Contact Us
For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@quotiq.com.
