# Quotiq: CPQ Research & Strategic Roadmap (2026)

## 1. Executive Summary
This report evaluates **Quotiq** against the 2026 Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) market standards. While Quotiq currently excels as a professional **Quoting & Invoicing tool**, it is currently at approximately **25-30% CPQ maturity**. To reach true enterprise CPQ status, Quotiq must evolve from a "static document generator" to an "intelligent GTM configuration engine."

---

## 2. 2026 CPQ Market Landscape & Competitors

### Top Enterprise Competitors
| Competitor | Core Strength | 2026 Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Salesforce Revenue Cloud** | CRM Integration | AI-powered pricing science & Revenue Lifecycle Management. |
| **DealHub** | SaaS & Hybrid Models | Agentic CPQ; Unified billing, usage metering, and rev-rec. |
| **Logik.io (ServiceNow)** | Headless Architecture | Blazing fast solving engine for complex hardware/software rules. |
| **PROS Smart CPQ** | Dynamic Pricing | AI-driven margin optimization and real-time market adjustments. |
| **Zuora** | Subscription Economy | Complex renewals, ramp deals, and consumption-based billing. |

### Key 2026 Trends
1. **Agentic CPQ**: AI agents that generate quotes from natural language prompts and historical win data.
2. **GTM Configuration**: Shifting focus from "product rules" to "commercial rules" (e.g., usage-based bundles).
3. **Headless CPQ**: Decoupling the pricing engine from the UI to support omnichannel (Portal, Sales, API).
4. **Unified Revenue**: Seamless flow from Quote -> Contract -> Billing -> Revenue Recognition.

## 4. Strategic Roadmap: The Path to CPQ Excellence

### Phase 1: Intelligent Quoting (Short Term - 0-3 Months)
- [ ] **Guided Selling**: Implement a "Questionnaire" mode to suggest products to sales reps.
- [ ] **Approval Workflows**: Add "Discount Thresholds" that trigger manager notifications.
- [ ] **Versioning**: Track "Quote Versions" and revision history (Completed).

### Phase 2: Configuration Engine (Medium Term - 3-9 Months)
- [ ] **Product Rules**: Implement a rules engine for product compatibility and dependencies.
- [ ] **Bundles**: Allow grouping products into "Smart Bundles" with predefined discounts.
- [ ] **Multi-Currency**: Full support for global quoting with real-time exchange rates.

### Phase 3: Agentic & Unified Revenue (Long Term - 9-18 Months)
- [ ] **QuotiqAI Agent**: AI-assisted quote generation and pricing optimization.
- [ ] **Subscription Billing**: Integrate recurring billing and usage-based tracking.
- [ ] **Headless API**: Expose the pricing engine via API for partner portals.

---

## 5. Conclusion
Quotiq is a robust foundation for invoicing. By focusing on **Commercial Configuration** (Pricing/Bundles) rather than just **Product Configuration**, Quotiq can carve a niche in the high-growth SaaS and Services market, competing with DealHub and Salesforce on agility rather than legacy complexity.
